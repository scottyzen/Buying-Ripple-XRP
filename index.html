Buying Ripple's XRP

A quick and easy guide to buying XRP

  1. First, sign up to Coinbase
  2. Once your coinbase account is verified you can buy Bitcoin ( BTC ) or Ethereum ( ETH )
  3. Next, you need to find an exchange that has BTC/XRP or ETH/XRP
  4. Here's two. HitBTC or Binance . Pick one and sign up.
  5. Now send either your BTC or ETH from your coinbase wallet to your exchange.
  6. Once your currency arrives you can exchange it for Ripple's XRP
