Buying Ripple's XRP

A quick and easy guide to buying XRP

  1. First, sign up to Coinbase
  2. Once your coinbase account is verified you can buy Ethereum ( ETH )
  3. Next, you need to find an exchange that has ETH/XRP
  4. Here's two. HitBTC or Binance . Pick one and sign up.
  5. Now send your ETH from your coinbase wallet to your exchange.
  6. It shouldn't take long for your ETH to arrive. Once it does you can exchange it for XRP.
